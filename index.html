About Me

Ashley Sweatt is a public educator for the city of Richmond and has been in the field of education since 2010. She earned a Bachelor’s of Science degree in Interdisciplinary Studies and a Master’s degree in Education from Old Dominion University in 2009 and 2010. She has had many successes as an educator. In 2011 Sweatt was named Henrico County’s New Teacher of the Year. She has also established and ran programs such as the Red Cross Club and Coding for Kids at her current school.

Ashley has two of her own businesses where she tutors students in math. After receiving her Event and Wedding Planner Certification in 2016, she also helps brides plan their big day!

Currently she is studying Coding at the University of Richmond to pursue a career in computer programming. Ashley Sweatt resides in Richmond, Virginia.
